What We Built

Capabilities that ship today.

Henok AI v1.0 isn't a demo. It's a production-grade platform built to think clearly, respond quickly, and stay out of your way.

⚡

Lightning Reasoning

A fine-tuned inference pipeline engineered for sub-second responses under real workloads, with graceful degradation at peak traffic.

🧠

Context That Sticks

Long-form memory and retrieval layers that stay coherent across sessions — no more "wait, what were we talking about?"

🛡️

Safety by Design

Policy guardrails, content filters, and red-team hardening baked in from the first commit, not bolted on at the end.

🎨

Multimodal Canvas

Text, image, and structured data flow through a unified representation, so the output matches how you actually think.

🔌

Real Integrations

First-class SDKs, REST + streaming APIs, and drop-in connectors for the tools you already use every day.

🌍

Built for Everyone

Localized for multiple languages on day one, with accessibility and inclusive defaults at the core of the experience.

The Journey

From blank canvas to v1.0.

The long road to launch, compressed into moments that mattered.

2025 · SPARK

The First Commit

A single repo, a single README, and a single wildly ambitious idea: build an AI that feels genuinely useful, not just impressive.

2025 · FOUNDATION

Core Architecture

Training pipelines, eval harnesses, and the first version of the reasoning stack came online. Everything broke. Everything got fixed.

2026 · Q1 · ALPHA

Internal Alpha

The first conversations with Henok actually made sense. A small circle of brave testers pushed it to its limits and documented every edge case.

2026 · Q1 · BETA

Closed Beta

Onboarded early partners. Refined the tone, trimmed the rough edges, and watched real users solve real problems with it.

2026 · APRIL · LAUNCH
